1. scrollToSection: A function to scroll to a specified section.

2. sectionId: The ID of the section to scroll to.

3. behavior: Defines how the scrolling will occur ("smooth" for smooth scrolling, "auto" for instant scrolling).

4. document.getElementById: Retrieves the section element by its ID.

5. scrollIntoView: Scrolls the page to make the section visible in the viewport.
